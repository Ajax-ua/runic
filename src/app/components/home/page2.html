Now, take a look at the following example…

1- Fehu, or the Wealth and Abundance Rune

Fehu is the first Rune, and its image is similar to the letter F. The Norse meaning of this word refers to cattle. The rune itself symbolizes the energy of life, all things that can be sustained and recycled and give birth to life. It is also linked with expansion, fresh starts, and sexual energy.

2- Uruz, or the Power Rune

Uruz, the second Rune, is represented by the letter U. It refers to Bison. The Rune symbolizes all that is strong, wild, and powerful. It is raw, unstoppable power that is usually beyond human control. If you do manage to harness it, you will have the opportunity to use this force to your advantage and achieve your goals. It is an incredible strength that urges you to conquer your fears and pushes you to succeed. Uruz can help you discover the path that you should take in certain life situations.

3- Thurisaz, or the Protective Rune

The third Rune is Thurisaz, represented by the sound “th”. Its Norse meaning is thorn. Like a thorn on a beautiful flower, Thurisaz protects you from outside forces harming your beautiful soul. The energy that it sends is both one of empowerment as well as chaos. Thurisaz can give you guidance on how to protect yourself from negative influences or powers that can potentially cause you problems. The Rune also warns you of possible challenges and helps you steer clear of disasters.
